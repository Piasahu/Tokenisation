Core DID Components

Self-Sovereign Identity (SSI)

Users maintain complete control over personal data and credentials, sharing only necessary information for transactions without relying on centralized authorities.

User-Centric

Verifiable Credentials

Cryptographically verifiable claims about identity attributes instantly validated by counterparties without third-party intermediaries.

Trustless

Wallet-Based Management

Credentials stored in user-controlled digital wallets, eliminating single points of failure and data breach risks.

Secure

Privacy Protection

Users control disclosure granularity, sharing only information necessary for specific transactions.

Privacy-First
